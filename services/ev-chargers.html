EV charger installation

Got an electric car, or thinking about one? Jake supplies and installs home EV chargers, set up safely and ready to go.

Charging at home overnight is the easiest and cheapest way to keep an EV topped up. Jake can set you up with a proper home charger so you wake up to a full battery, no more hunting for a public charger.

What's involved

  • Advice on the right charger for your car
  • Supply of quality home chargers
  • Safe install in the garage or carport
  • A check that your board can handle it
  • Dedicated, protected circuit
  • Tidy, certified work

Done safely, the first time

EV chargers draw a fair bit of power, so they need to be installed properly on the right circuit. Jake will check your switchboard can cope and set everything up safely, so you can charge with total peace of mind.

Future-proof your home

Even if the EV is still on the wish list, getting set up now adds value and saves hassle later. Give Jake a call to talk through your options.

Lighting & LED upgrades

From a single new downlight to lighting a whole new home, Jake makes your place brighter, warmer and cheaper to run.

Good lighting changes how a home feels. Swapping tired old fittings for modern LEDs not only looks great, it can cut your power bill too, because LEDs use a fraction of the energy and last for years.

Lighting Jake can help with

  • LED downlight upgrades
  • New light fittings and pendants
  • Dimmers and smart switches
  • Outdoor and deck lighting
  • Sensor and security lighting
  • Shed, garage and sleepout lighting

Save on your power bill

If your home still runs old halogen downlights, you're paying more than you need to and they run hot. Jake can swap them for efficient LEDs that give a nicer light and keep your bill down.

Done the way you want it

Jake will talk through the look you're after, the right brightness for each room and where switches make the most sense, then get it installed neatly with no mess left behind.

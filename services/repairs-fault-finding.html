Electrical repairs & fault finding

Something not working right? Jake tracks down the problem and fixes it safely, so your home is back to normal fast.

Electrical faults are frustrating and, if they're left alone, they can be dangerous too. Jake has the gear and the know-how to find the root cause, not just patch over it. He'll explain what went wrong in plain English and sort it properly.

What Jake can fix

  • Tripping breakers and blown fuses
  • Dead or sparking power points
  • Faulty switches and dimmers
  • Flickering or failing lights
  • Hot water and appliance circuits
  • General safety checks and testing

Safe, honest and tidy

If something's not safe, Jake will tell you straight and won't do work you don't need. You'll get a clear price before he starts, and the job left clean and tidy when he's done.

Worried it might be urgent?

If you can smell burning, see sparks or have lost power to part of the house, switch it off at the board and give Jake a call. He'll talk you through what to do and get to you as soon as he can.

Switchboard upgrades

Still got an old fuse board? Jake can upgrade it to a modern switchboard with RCD safety switches to protect your whole family.

Your switchboard is the heart of your home's electrical system. Older boards with ceramic fuses don't offer the protection modern ones do, and they can struggle with the demands of today's appliances, heat pumps and EV chargers.

Why upgrade your switchboard

  • RCD safety switches that protect against shocks
  • Handles modern appliances and heat pumps
  • Easy to reset, no more fiddly fuse wire
  • Room to add new circuits down the track
  • Peace of mind for the whole household
  • Often needed for insurance and new builds

Done safely and signed off

Jake will assess your current board, recommend what you actually need, and do the upgrade to code with the proper certification. You'll know your home is safe and protected.

Not sure if you need one?

If your board has old ceramic fuses, no safety switches, or it trips often, it's worth a look. Give Jake a call and he'll take a look and give you honest advice, no pressure.

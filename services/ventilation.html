Home ventilation systems

Fresh, dry, healthy air all year round. We supply and install home ventilation systems that tackle condensation, damp and stuffy rooms across Christchurch and Canterbury.

Canterbury homes get cold, damp winters, and moisture builds up fast. A good ventilation system pushes out stale, damp air and brings in drier, filtered air, so you get fewer foggy windows, less mould and a warmer-feeling home that is cheaper to heat.

What's involved

  • Advice on the right system for your home
  • Positive-pressure and heat-recovery options
  • Tidy install with all the electrical done properly
  • Extractor fans for bathrooms and kitchens
  • Quality, trusted brands
  • A clear walk-through of how to use it

A drier, healthier home

Damp air is harder to heat and can lead to mould and musty smells. Ventilation keeps the moisture moving out so your home feels warmer, smells fresher and is healthier for the whole family.

Pairs well with a heat pump

Ventilation and heating work best together. If you're sorting one, it's worth asking about the other. Give us a call and we'll talk through what suits your home.
